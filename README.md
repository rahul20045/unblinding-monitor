 Unblinding Monitor

Real-time surveillance presentation for clinical trial unblinding events — ensuring data integrity, regulatory compliance, and endpoint validity.

**Protocol:** STU-2024-087 | **Phase:** III, Multi-Center | **Enrolled:** 1,240 subjects across 42 sites

---
## Overview

Unblinding — the premature revelation of a participant's treatment assignment — is one of the most critical risks to clinical trial integrity. Even a single event can introduce systematic bias, compromise endpoints, and trigger regulatory findings.

This presentation provides a structured, data-driven view of all unblinding events, trends, root causes, site-level performance, risk assessment, and actionable next steps for the sponsor review cycle (Q1 2025).
